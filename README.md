# BitPlace

BitPlace is a simple on-chain version of r/place, mainly built as a first practice project.

Each address can paint up to 10 pixels for free (aside from gas) per day. Beyond that, you may pay a small fee to paint as many additional pixels as you want. These fees go into a pot, which you have a chance of winning 75% of whenever you paint your 11th pixel each day.\*

Have fun!

## FAQ

### Why does it only roll for a lottery win on the 11th paint?

_As with pretty much every "feature" here, it was primarily motivated by me just wanted to explore new logic, syntax, ideas, etc. just for the practice. This way was "harder" to build._
Then a secondary motivation was making things like UI/UX feel somewhat polished without using any templates or libraries, and under the hood mechanics like the lottery be at least somewhat logical. While this system is still gamed incredibly easily, **this way it's not as easy as someone just writting a script to paint over and over, block after block, until they win.**

### Why aren't there more user-friendly painting tools, like on other pixel boards?

Again, just because this isn't meant to be a serious long-term project in any capacity. **However**, if this somehow gains some usage, I would be open to updating it further.
